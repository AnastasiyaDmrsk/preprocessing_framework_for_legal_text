The process begins with a start event.

The first step is to "Ensure design and manufacturing compliance."
Following this, the process moves to "Establish and maintain risk management system."
Next, "Conduct clinical evaluation including PMCF" is performed.

After the clinical evaluation, there is a decision point.
If the device is a "Custom-made device," the process proceeds to "Prepare and maintain documentation for custom-made devices."
Then, "Comply with UDI and registration obligations" is carried out.
This is followed by "Keep technical documentation, EU declaration of conformity, and certificates available."
Subsequently, "Ensure series production conformity and update QMS" is done.
The process then involves "Implement and update post-market surveillance system."
After that, "Ensure device is accompanied by required information."

At this stage, there is a check for device conformity.
If the "Device not in conformity," the process involves "Take corrective action, inform distributors, and authorities if serious risk."
If the "Device in conformity," the process continues.

Regardless of the conformity status, the process eventually leads to "Establish system for recording and reporting incidents and corrective actions."
Following this, "Provide information and documentation to competent authorities upon request."

Here, another decision point arises based on the manufacturer's cooperation.
If the "Manufacturer fails to cooperate or provides incomplete/incorrect info," the "Competent authority takes measures to prohibit or restrict device availability."
If the "Manufacturer cooperates or provides complete and correct info," the process proceeds to "Facilitate provision of information to injured parties if device caused damage."

This leads to a further branching based on the nature of the information.
If the information is "Information ordinarily dealt with in legal proceedings," the process continues.
If the information is "Disclosure not ordinarily dealt with in legal proceedings," the process involves "Identify legal or natural person designing/manufacturing device."
Following this, "Ensure sufficient financial coverage for potential liability."

The process then converges and continues from the "Manufacturer cooperates or provides complete and correct info" path.

The process then converges again and continues from the "Provide information and documentation to competent authorities upon request" step.
