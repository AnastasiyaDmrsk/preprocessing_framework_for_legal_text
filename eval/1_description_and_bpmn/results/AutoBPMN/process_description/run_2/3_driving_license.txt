The process begins with a start event. The first step is to "Determine Licence Category". After this determination, the process branches based on the licence category.

If the category is "AM Category", the process proceeds to a decision point. If the "AM Category" requires a medical examination, the process moves to "Conduct Medical Examination", followed by "Assess Fitness to Drive". After assessing fitness, the process reaches a convergence point. If the "AM Category" does not require a medical examination, the process also reaches this convergence point.

From this convergence point, the process further branches based on whether it's a "Renewal", "First Issuance", or another type of application.

If it's a "Renewal" and the "AM Category" is required, the process involves "Conduct Medical Examination" and "Assess Fitness to Drive", leading to a convergence point. If it's a "Renewal" and the "AM Category" is not required, it directly leads to the same convergence point.

If it's a "First Issuance" and the "AM Category" is required, the process involves "Conduct Medical Examination" and "Assess Fitness to Drive", leading to a convergence point. If it's a "First Issuance" and the "AM Category" is not required, it also leads to this convergence point.

The process then considers other licence categories. If the category is "C, CE, C1, C1E, D, D1, DE, D1E", the process involves "Conduct Medical Examination" and "Assess Fitness to Drive", leading to a convergence point.

If the category falls under "Other Categories", the process again branches based on "Renewal" or "First Issuance".

For "Other Categories" with "Renewal", if the "AM Category" is required, the process includes "Conduct Medical Examination" and "Assess Fitness to Drive", leading to a convergence point. If the "AM Category" is not required, it also leads to this convergence point.

For "Other Categories" with "First Issuance", if the "AM Category" is required, the process includes "Conduct Medical Examination" and "Assess Fitness to Drive", leading to a convergence point. If the "AM Category" is not required, it also leads to this convergence point.

After all these branches converge, there is a final decision point. If a "Medical Condition Identified", the process moves to "Conduct Medical Examination". If "No Medical Condition Identified", it bypasses this examination.

Finally, regardless of the medical examination outcome, the process concludes with the task "Issue or Renew Licence", followed by an end event.
