The process begins with a starting event. Following this, Member States undertake actions. After these actions, a decision point is reached. If SMEs are eligible for priority access, they are provided with priority access to AI Regulatory Sandboxes. If they are other SMEs, they are provided with access to AI Regulatory Sandboxes. Both paths then converge at another decision point. From this point, awareness and training for SMEs are organized. Subsequently, communication channels for SMEs are utilized. This is followed by facilitating SME participation in standardization. Next, SME interests are considered in conformity assessment fees. Then, the AI Office takes actions. These actions include providing standardized templates. Following this, a single information platform is developed. Subsequently, communication campaigns are organized. Finally, best practices in public procurement are evaluated and promoted, leading to the end of the process.
