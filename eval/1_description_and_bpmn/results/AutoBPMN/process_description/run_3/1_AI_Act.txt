The process begins with a starting event. Following this, Member States undertake actions. After these actions, a decision point is reached. If the condition "Eligible for priority access" is met, priority access to AI Regulatory Sandboxes is provided. If the condition "Other SMEs" is met, access to AI Regulatory Sandboxes is provided. Both paths then converge at another decision point.

From this point, the process continues with organizing awareness and training for SMEs. Subsequently, communication channels are utilized for SMEs. This is followed by facilitating SME participation in standardization. Next, SME interests are considered in conformity assessment fees.

After these steps, the AI Office takes actions. This includes providing standardized templates. Then, a single information platform is developed. Following this, communication campaigns are organized. The process concludes with evaluating and promoting best practices in public procurement, leading to the end event.
