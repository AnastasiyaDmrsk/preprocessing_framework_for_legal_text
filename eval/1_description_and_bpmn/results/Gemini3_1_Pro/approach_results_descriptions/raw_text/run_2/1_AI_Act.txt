The process contains the following actors: SME, Member State, Board, AI Office.

The process starts with the need to implement measures for providers and deployers.

The process for the SME starts.
SME may Share interests and needs if required.
SME shall transmit interests and needs to the Member State.
The process for the SME ends.

The process for the Member State starts.
Member State shall carry out the following 4 activities in parallel:

* Member State shall assess whether SMEs have a registered office or a branch in the Union and fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria. If SMEs have a registered office or a branch in the Union and fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria, Member State shall Provide SMEs with priority access. Member State shall assess whether other SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria. If other SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria, Member State shall not Preclude other SMEs from access to the AI regulatory sandbox.
* Member State shall Organise specific activities.
* Member State shall Utilise existing dedicated channels. Member State shall assess whether it is appropriate. If it is appropriate, Member State shall Establish new channels. Member State shall Provide advice. Member State shall Respond to queries.
* Member State shall Facilitate the participation of SMEs in the development process.
All 4 activities shall be completed before the next step.
Upon receiving interests and needs, Member State shall Take into account the specific interests and needs.
Member State shall Set the fees for conformity assessment.
The process for the Member State ends.

The process for the Board starts.
Board may Request standardised templates if required.
Board shall transmit the request for standardised templates to the AI Office.
The process for the Board ends.

The process for the AI Office starts.
AI Office shall carry out the following 4 activities in parallel:

* Upon receiving the request for standardised templates, AI Office shall assess whether templates are specified by the Board in its request. If templates are specified by the Board in its request, AI Office shall Provide standardised templates.
* AI Office shall Develop a single information platform. AI Office shall Maintain a single information platform.
* AI Office shall Organise appropriate communication campaigns.
* AI Office shall Evaluate the convergence of best practices. AI Office shall Promote the convergence of best practices.
All 4 activities shall be completed before the next step.
The process for the AI Office ends.
