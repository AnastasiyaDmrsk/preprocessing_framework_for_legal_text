The process contains the following actors: Member State, SME, Board, AI Office.

The process starts with the implementation of measures for providers and deployers, simultaneously triggering the processes for the Member State, SME, Board, and AI Office.

The Member State shall carry out the following 4 activities in parallel:

* The Member State shall assess whether having a registered office or a branch in the Union and if SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria. If having a registered office or a branch in the Union and if SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria, the Member State shall provide SMEs with priority access. The Member State shall assess whether other SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria. If other SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria, the Member State shall not preclude other SMEs from access to the AI regulatory sandbox.
* The Member State shall organise specific activities.
* The Member State shall utilise existing dedicated channels. The Member State may establish new channels if appropriate. The Member State shall provide advice. The Member State shall respond to queries.
* The Member State shall facilitate the participation of SMEs in the development process.
All 4 activities shall be completed before the next step.
Upon receiving interests and needs from the SME, the Member State shall take into account the specific interests and needs. The Member State shall set the fees for conformity assessment.
The process for the Member State ends with the conformity assessment fees set end event.

The process for the SME starts.
The SME may share interests and needs if required.
The SME shall transmit interests and needs to the Member State.
The process for the SME ends.

The process for the Board starts.
The Board may request standardised templates if required.
The Board shall transmit the request for standardised templates to the AI Office.
The process for the Board ends.

The process for the AI Office starts.
The AI Office shall carry out the following 4 activities in parallel:

* Upon receiving the request for standardised templates from the Board, the AI Office shall assess whether as specified by the Board in its request. If as specified by the Board in its request, the AI Office shall provide standardised templates.
* The AI Office shall develop a single information platform. The AI Office shall maintain a single information platform.
* The AI Office shall organise appropriate communication campaigns.
* The AI Office shall evaluate the convergence of best practices. The AI Office shall promote the convergence of best practices.
All 4 activities shall be completed before the process ends.
The process for the AI Office ends.
