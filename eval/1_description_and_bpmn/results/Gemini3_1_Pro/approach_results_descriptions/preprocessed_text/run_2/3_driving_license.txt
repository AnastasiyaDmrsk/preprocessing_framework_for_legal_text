The process contains the following actors: Applicant, Member State, Commission.

The process starts with an application for the issuance or renewal of a driving licence.

Applicant shall undergo a medical examination before a driving licence is issued for the first time or renewed or for driving licences of categories C, CE, C1, C1E, D, D1, DE or D1E or for category AM, except where for category AM, the medical examination is required only if so required by the Member State, and except where alternative measures are used.
Applicant shall fill in a self-assessment form for driving licence categories AM, A, A1, A2, B, B1 and BE if required instead of a medical examination to the extent not otherwise provided for by Annex III.
The process for the Applicant ends with the completion of fitness evaluation end event.

The process for the Member State starts.
Member State shall ensure that applicants undergo a medical examination before a driving licence is issued for the first time, except where it applies to the issuance of driving licences in category AM only if so required by the Member State in accordance with Article 10(1), point (b).
Member State shall require a medical examination for driving licences of categories C, CE, C1, C1E, D, D1, DE or D1E or for category AM, except where for category AM, the medical examination may be required.
Member State shall carry out the following 2 activities in parallel:

* Member State may require the applicant to fill in a self-assessment form for driving licence categories AM, A, A1, A2, B, B1 and BE notwithstanding paragraphs 1 and 2 to the extent not otherwise provided for by Annex III.
* Member State may establish a national system of assessment of fitness to drive for driving licence categories AM, A, A1, A2, B, B1 and BE if required instead of a medical examination applicable after the licence has been issued following a prior medical examination or self-assessment.
All 2 activities shall be completed before further assessment.
Member State may provide for appropriate measures to address non-compliance for non-compliance with the requirement to fill in a self-assessment form or for knowingly providing information in the self-assessment form that is incorrect or incomplete or for failing to meet any requirement established in accordance with paragraph 3, point (b).
Member State may apply the national system to monitor if the Member State has established a national system of assessment of fitness to drive.
Member State shall assess whether the applicant is likely to have the medical conditions. If the applicant is likely to have the medical conditions, Member State shall ensure that the applicant undergoes a medical examination.
Member State may take measures to raise awareness if awareness is required.
Member State may take measures to improve the knowledge of the health sector if knowledge improvement is required.
Member State may adopt guidelines for medical practitioners if adopting of guidelines is required.
Member State shall assess whether the Member State adopts guidelines for medical practitioners. If the Member State adopts guidelines for medical practitioners, Member State shall transmit guidelines adopted to the Commission.
Member State may develop public awareness campaigns if awareness campaigns are required.
Member State shall assess whether the Member State develops public awareness campaigns. If the Member State develops public awareness campaigns, Member State shall transmit any public awareness campaigns developed to the Commission.
Member State may set standards stricter than those set out in Annex III if stricter standards are required.
The process for the Member State ends with the completion of licensing and awareness activities end event.

The process for the Commission starts.
Upon receiving guidelines adopted, Commission shall make available to the other Member States the guidelines adopted.
Upon receiving any public awareness campaigns developed, Commission shall make available to the other Member States the information.
The process for the Commission ends with the information distribution end event.
