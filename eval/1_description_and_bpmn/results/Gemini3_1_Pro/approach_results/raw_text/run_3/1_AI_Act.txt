
The process contains the following actors: Member State, SME, Board, AI Office.

The process starts with the need to implement measures for providers and deployers, simultaneously triggering the subprocesses for the Member State, SME, Board, and AI Office.

The process for the SME starts.
The SME may share interests and needs if required.
The SME shall transmit interests and needs to the Member State.
The process for the SME ends.

The process for the Board starts.
The Board may request standardised templates if required.
The Board shall transmit the request for standardised templates to the AI Office.
The process for the Board ends.

The process for the Member State starts.
The Member State shall carry out the following 4 activities in parallel:

* The Member State shall assess whether SMEs having a registered office or a branch in the Union fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria. If SMEs having a registered office or a branch in the Union fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria is true, the Member State shall provide SMEs with priority access. The Member State shall assess whether other SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria. If other SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria is true, the Member State shall not preclude other SMEs from access to the AI regulatory sandbox.
* The Member State shall organise specific activities.
* The Member State shall utilise existing dedicated channels. The Member State shall assess whether appropriate. If appropriate is true, the Member State shall establish new channels. The Member State shall provide advice. The Member State shall respond to queries.
* The Member State shall facilitate the participation of SMEs in the development process.
All 4 activities shall be completed before taking into account the specific interests and needs.
Upon receiving interests and needs, the Member State shall take into account the specific interests and needs.
The Member State shall set the fees for conformity assessment.
The process for the Member State ends.

The process for the AI Office starts.
The AI Office shall carry out the following 4 activities in parallel:

* Upon receiving the request for standardised templates, the AI Office shall assess whether as specified by the Board in its request. If as specified by the Board in its request is true, the AI Office shall provide standardised templates.
* The AI Office shall develop a single information platform. The AI Office shall maintain a single information platform.
* The AI Office shall organise appropriate communication campaigns.
* The AI Office shall evaluate the convergence of best practices. The AI Office shall promote the convergence of best practices.
All 4 activities shall be completed before the process ends.
The process for the AI Office ends.
