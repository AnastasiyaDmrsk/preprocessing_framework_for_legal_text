The process starts with "Member States Actions." After that, four activities occur in parallel: "Priority Access to AI Regulatory Sandboxes," "Awareness Raising and Training Activities," "Utilize Existing Dedicated Channels," and "Facilitate Participation in Standardisation." Once all four activities are completed, the process continues with "Consider SME Interests in Conformity Assessment Fees," followed by "AI Office Actions." Subsequently, four more activities are executed in parallel: "Provide Standardised Templates," "Develop and Maintain Information Platform," "Organise Communication Campaigns," and "Evaluate and Promote Convergence of Best Practices." Finally, after all these activities are finished, the process ends.
