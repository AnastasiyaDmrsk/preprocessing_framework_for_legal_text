The process contains the following actors: Member State, AI Office, SME, and Board.

The process starts with two parallel subprocesses: one for Member State and one for AI Office.

Following activities occur in parallel for Member State:
-	Member State shall facilitate the participation of SMEs in the development process;
-	Member State shall organise specific awareness raising and training activities;
-	Member State shall utilise existing dedicated channels OR Member State shall establish new channels if no exist. After that, SME may share interests and needs and following, Member State shall take into account the specific interests and needs;
-	Member State shall provide SMEs with priority access to the AI regulatory sandboxes if SMEs fulfil the eligibility conditions and selection criteria.

Following activities occur in parallel for AI Office:
-	Board shall request standardised templates and after that AI Office shall provide standardised templates;
-	AI Office shall develop a single information platform and then maintain a single information platform;
-	AI Office shall organise appropriate communication campaigns;
-	AI Office shall evaluate the convergence of best practices. Then, AI Office shall promote the convergence of best practices.

Finally, the two initial subprocesses merge, and the process ends.
